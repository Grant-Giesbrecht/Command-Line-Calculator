---------------------------------------------------------
--------------------- CLC RECORD HELP -------------------

Topic Description:
    The record catalogues all commands entered into CLC. It allows
    the user to review and document his or her work. The record can
    be saved to a file with the ‘SVRCD’ command, viewed with ‘PRCD’
    command, or cleared with ‘CLRCD’.

Additional Help:
    PRCD - Print record
    SVRCD - Save record
    CLRCD - Clear record
