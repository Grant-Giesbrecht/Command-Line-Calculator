---------------------------------------------------------
--------------------- CLC SVRCD HELP --------------------

Description:
    Saves the record to a text file.

Flags:
    -H || -HOME: Append home directory to filename beginning
    -SD || -SAVE_DIR: Append SAVE_DIR directory to filename beginning
    [NO FLAG]: Interprets following words as filename, spaces are eliminated

Additional Help:
    CLRCD - Clear record
    PRCD - Print record
    RECORD - (TOPIC) The calculator record
